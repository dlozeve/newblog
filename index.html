--- title: Welcome! ---

I am an Engineering Manager at Doctrine, where we develop productivity tools to help lawyers analyze and write their private documents.

I was previously a Research Engineer and Project Manager at Sysnav, where I worked on developing algorithms to help physicians measure the progress of neuromuscular diseases. We used data from inertial sensors to extract meaningful variables for pharmaceutical studies.

Before that, I was a Data Scientist at Mindsay, working on natural language understanding for chatbots.
